Kidney-Bladder System · Patterns

Heart / Kidney Disharmony

Multi-Organ Patterns
Key Symptoms & Clinical Findings

Insomnia with difficulty falling and staying asleep, anxiety, palpitations, night sweats, dizziness, tinnitus, low back soreness, dream-disturbed sleep, and nocturnal emissions — the classic "Fire and Water fail to communicate" picture.

Tongue

Red tongue, especially at the tip, with little coating.

Pulse

Thready (Xi) and Rapid (Shuo), weak at both Chi (Kidney) positions and slightly rapid at the left Cun (Heart) position.
