Yang ExcessHot / Inflammatory

Full heat, hyperinflammatory, hypermetabolic — acute onset, resolves with clearing/cooling.

Yang DeficiencyCold / Hypometabolic

Reduced mitochondrial output, impaired thermogenesis — constitutional or chronic, resolves with warming/tonifying.

Qi ExcessTense / Autonomic Excess

Autonomic/neurogenic hyperreactivity — spasmodic, stress-responsive, migratory.

Qi DeficiencyLax / Autonomic Insufficiency

Autonomic and functional weakness — fatigue, poor tone, poor immune resilience.

Blood ExcessStagnant / Congestive

Fixed, congestive, hyperviscous states. Not currently mapped for Lung in this framework (Blood patterns are primarily Liver/Heart/Spleen territory).

Blood DeficiencyDeficient / Malnourished

Nutritive depletion — pale, thin, poorly perfused. Not currently mapped for Lung in this framework.

Yin ExcessDamp / Fluid Accumulation

Fluid/mucus accumulation, impaired clearance — heaviness, phlegm, congestion.

Yin DeficiencyDry / Fluid Depleted

Fluid-structural depletion — dryness, atrophy, empty heat; acute (external) or chronic (internal) forms.
